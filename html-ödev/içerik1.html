Köfteci İsmail

When it comes to Adapazarı, the first name that comes to mind is Köfteci İsmail. Since its establishment in 1928, the restaurant has combined the concept of köfte with delicious taste and affordable prices, making it not only a trusted brand in Adapazarı but also across Turkey. Despite the management passing to the third generation, Köfteci İsmail still preserves its traditional methods of preparing köfte, maintaining the authenticity and quality that has made it a beloved institution.

Morina Balık Evi

Morina Balık Evi in Sakarya is a well-known restaurant famous for its fresh seafood and fish dishes. Popular among both locals and visitors, this establishment is celebrated for its warm atmosphere and fast service. The menu features a variety of options, with the cod fish being the highlight, offering both grilled and steamed preparations for a delicious dining experience. Located centrally, the restaurant is an ideal choice for seafood lovers in Adapazarı. With reasonable prices for the quality, Morina Balık Evi is a must-visit spot for those seeking fresh fish and seafood in the city.

Tunatan

Köfteci Hacı Bekir

Köfteci Hacı Bekir in Sakarya is a historic restaurant that offers traditional Turkish cuisine with a modern touch. Operating since the 1950s, the restaurant is particularly famous for its delicious chicken and meat köfte. Located close to the city center, it provides guests with both tasty meals and a warm atmosphere. The nostalgic decor inside reflects the rich history of this long-established venue. If you find yourself in Sakarya, be sure to enjoy a satisfying meal with köfte and various appetizers. One of the restaurant’s standout features is the quick service, making it perfect for those on the go. It's an ideal spot for a delightful meal with family or friends.

Qırım Han

With its focus on high-quality ingredients and classic cooking techniques, Qırımhan Sakarya offers an unforgettable döner experience in a cozy and inviting setting. Saklı Göl Restaurant

Saklı Göl Restaurant is a charming and tranquil dining venue nestled in the heart of nature, offering a delightful experience for those seeking a combination of delicious food and scenic surroundings. The restaurant is situated near the beautiful Hidden Lake, allowing guests to enjoy panoramic views of the serene lake and lush greenery as they dine. Known for its warm and welcoming atmosphere, Saklı Göl Restaurant serves a variety of traditional Turkish dishes, with a focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients. The menu includes a mix of regional specialties and classic Turkish favorites, all prepared with care and attention to detail. Whether you're looking for a peaceful place to enjoy a meal with family or friends, or a romantic setting for a special occasion, Saklı Göl Restaurant offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and exceptional cuisine. It’s an ideal choice for anyone wanting to savor authentic flavors while immersing themselves in the tranquility of nature.

Dönerci Ömer

Dönerci Ömer is a popular and beloved local eatery in Sakarya, famous for its exceptional döner kebab. Known for its casual, yet inviting atmosphere, this restaurant offers a laid-back dining experience, perfect for those craving authentic Turkish fast food. The döner is prepared with high-quality meat, perfectly seasoned and served with fresh vegetables and warm pita or bread. With its friendly service and tasty offerings, Dönerci Ömer is a must-try for döner enthusiasts looking to experience a true taste of Turkish street food in a comfortable, no-frills setting. Dönerci Ömer is a place that Vedat Milor also visited.
