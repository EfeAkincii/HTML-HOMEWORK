Poyrazlar Lake Nature Park

Poyrazlar Lake Picnic Area is a peaceful spot in Poyrazlar Lake Nature Park, Sakarya. It offers picnic tables, barbecue areas, and shaded spaces with beautiful lake views. Visitors can enjoy fishing, cycling, hiking, and horse riding, making it a great outdoor retreat. With its clean and tranquil atmosphere, it's perfect for a relaxing getaway in nature.

Maden Stream and Waterfall Picnic Area

Madenler Stream and Waterfall Picnic Area in Sakarya, Turkey, is a scenic retreat with lush forests, streams, and waterfalls. It offers a cool atmosphere, ideal for picnicking, hiking, and nature photography. Visitors can enjoy fresh air, local trout dishes, and peaceful surroundings, making it a perfect outdoor escape.

Acarlar Longoz

Acarlar Longoz Picnic Area in Sakarya, Turkey, offers a peaceful retreat with lush greenery, wooden tables, and barbecue spots. Visitors can enjoy boat tours, hiking, and birdwatching, surrounded by a rich ecosystem and scenic water views. It’s a perfect spot for relaxation in nature.

Karasu Beach Picnic Area

Karasu SBeach Picnic Area is a beautiful seaside park located along the Black Sea coast. It features long walking and cycling paths with a stunning sea view. Visitors can enjoy sandy beaches, picnic areas, and open-air cafes. The park also has playgrounds and recreational spots for families and children. It’s perfect for watching sunsets and relaxing by the sea. Karasu Sahil Park is a popular destination for both locals and tourists during summer.

Kent Park

Kent Park in Sakarya is a peaceful urban retreat with lush greenery, walking paths, and a scenic lake. Covering 160,000 square meters, it was transformed from an industrial site into a vibrant public space. The park features picnic areas, playgrounds, and sports facilities, making it ideal for relaxation and outdoor activities. With its serene atmosphere and natural beauty, Kent Park is a favorite spot for both locals and visitors.

Sakarya Park

Sakarya Park is a scenic urban park located in Erenler, Sakarya. It offers green spaces, walking paths, and recreational areas, making it a great spot for relaxation and outdoor activities. The park features cafés, playgrounds, and picnic areas, providing a peaceful environment for families and visitors. With its serene atmosphere and well-maintained landscape, Sakarya Park is a popular destination for both locals and tourists.
