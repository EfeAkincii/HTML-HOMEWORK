Mono Cafe

Mono Cafe, located in Adapazarı, Sakarya, is a versatile venue offering a cozy atmosphere for both casual dining and special events. Known for its diverse menu, the café serves a variety of dishes ranging from hearty meals to delightful desserts like tiramisu. The establishment is also popular for hosting events such as post-wedding dinners, graduation parties, and corporate gatherings, accommodating up to 250 guests. With its warm ambiance and attentive service, Mono Cafe provides a welcoming environment for all occasions. Whether you're looking for a place to enjoy a meal or celebrate a special event, Mono Cafe stands out as a notable spot in Sakarya.

Jungle Coffee & Tea

Jungle Coffee & Tea, located in Serdivan, Sakarya, offers a cozy and inviting atmosphere for coffee and tea enthusiasts. The café features a variety of beverages and desserts, making it a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. With its comfortable seating and warm ambiance, it's an ideal place to relax or catch up with friends. The staff is known for their friendly service, enhancing the overall experience. Whether you're in the mood for a hot cup of coffee or a refreshing tea, Jungle Coffee & Tea provides a delightful setting. It's a must-visit destination for those exploring the café scene in Serdivan.

Uğurlu Cafe

Uğurlu Dükkan Cafe is a cozy spot in Sapanca’s Kırkpınar area, combining homemade food with local handmade products. Founded by a couple who moved from Istanbul, the café serves delicious items like brownies, manti, and specialty coffee. Inside, there’s also a boutique featuring crafts by local women. Its warm atmosphere and unique concept make it a popular stop for both tourists and locals. Ideal for a relaxing break or gift shopping. A true hidden gem in Sapanca.

Göynüklü Ömer Usta

Göynüklü Ömer Usta Dondurmaları is a well-known dessert stop in Serdivan, offering traditional Turkish ice cream since 1978. Made with fresh cow’s milk, natural salep, and real fruits, their ice cream is completely free of additives. The shop has a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, perfect for enjoying sweet treats. In addition to ice cream, they serve popular Turkish desserts like künefe, katmer, and irmik helvası. The quality and authenticity make it a favorite among locals and visitors alike. It’s an ideal place to experience classic Turkish flavors.
